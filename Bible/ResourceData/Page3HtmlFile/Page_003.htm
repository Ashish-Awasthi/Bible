The Bible as an eLuminated app

The purpose of this eLume is to introduce people
to the love and light of God as it is revealed in Scripture.

To learn more, explore, or share with others,
tap or pull down the red ribbon.

In the Hebrew Bible, sometimes what is heard
is slightly different from what is written.

To hear the Word, tap the Hebrew letter
in the story pages that follow.

See if you can spot when the narration differs from the text.
