The storytellers

Author and illustrator

Benjamin Morse grew up in
Connecticut and Virginia
before studying religion and art
history at Vassar College.

He later earned a master’s in
biblical interpretation at Oxford
University and a master’s
in modern art at the Courtauld
Institute in London.

In 2008 he was awarded a PhD
from Glasgow University for a
dissertation on the Hebrew Bible’s
aesthetic diversity.

Morse with one of his four goddaughters.
This book was inspired by his first
and took eight years to make.

He lives in New York.

Narrator

Allison Hancock studied acting at the
Bristol Old Vic Theatre School.

She has performed throughout the UK
in repertory theatre, radio and
television, and on the London Fringe.

After the birth of her two daughters,
she began teaching speech and drama.
Her private clients and classes
in five West London schools include
students aged 4 to 40.

She is a regular reader in her church in
Isleworth, South London.

Recorded at Sans Walk Studios, London, by Nicholas Jones of Strathmore
