Generation after generation failed to honor
their commitment to God.

But all of the prophets’ predictions eventually came true.

God sent the Babylonians to conquer the land and to
take the king—and all the king’s princes, prophets, and priests—
to be prisoners in Babylon.

This was the beginning of a long time away from home
called the Exile.

Jerusalem meanwhile was an utter wasteland.

The Babylonians had toppled the temple and the city walls,
leaving only the poorest behind to clean up the rubble.
