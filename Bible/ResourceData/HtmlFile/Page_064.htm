Then there was a man from the land of Uz, a foreigner
named Job, who lost his family and his fortune.

His friends were hardly a comfort.

They insisted his misery must have
been his own fault—
a punishment from God.

But Job had done nothing wrong.

God finally spoke to Job
from a whirlwind,
showing him all the things in the
universe that had been too wonderful
for him to understand prior to this.

And Job took back some of the
things he had said in anger
to his friends about God.

Then God restored to Job
twice as much as he had had before ...
which is a little bit like what happened to Israel next.
