Samson was stronger than any man around.

He ate only healthy foods,
he drank no wine,
and he never cut
his divine hair.

He did not know his lover Delilah was
spying on him for the Philistines.

She discovered the secret to his power.

While he was sleeping,
Delilah instructed a servant to shave off
all seven of Samson’s ever so long locks.

And Samson woke up weak.

But as his hair grew back,
so did his
strength
return.

He pushed over the pillars of the Philistine temple with nothing
but his bare hands!   It was a fitting finale.
