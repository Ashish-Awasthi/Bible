In Egypt Joseph’s dreams came true as his wisdom led him
from prison rags to the princely life.

When famine hit the
land of Canaan,
the brothers went down to
Egypt in search of food.

Fortunately Joseph had advised his
friend, the Pharaoh, to save plenty of it.

Bowing and begging before a man
they were told could help them,
Jacob’s sons had no idea
this was their long-lost brother.

Joseph finally told them who he was
and saved his father Israel’s
family from starving.
