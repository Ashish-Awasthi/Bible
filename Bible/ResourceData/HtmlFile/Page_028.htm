Moses was very afraid at first,
especially when he heard what God wanted him to do:

“I will send you to Pharaoh
so that you may bring forth
my people
the children of Israel
out of the land of Egypt.”

Moses knew that Pharaoh would on no account let the people go ...
and that he would probably have Moses
thrown into prison for the rest of his life.

But after some persuasion, he agreed to go back.

God had chosen him to lead the people out of Egypt,
and His decision was final.
