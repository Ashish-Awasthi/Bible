Two centuries later, the Greeks conquered the Persians.

Their civilization did not always practice compassion.

The Greeks demanded that
the people of Israel bow down
their many gods... or die!

Many Jews refused to betray
their one true God,
despite the threat of death.

Daniel’s faith was so strong,
he survived being thrown
into a fiery furnace.

Even the jaws of hungry lions
could not convert him, and he
passed through their den unscathed.

God was somehow there no matter how trying the times.
