But on they went.

Adam and Eve’s next son Seth lived until he was 912.

Seth’s son Enosh made it to 905.

But it was Seth’s great-great-great-great-grandson Methuselah

who survived the longest of them all, reaching the ripe old age of 969.

Though giants are said to have

roamed the earth then,

people still thought of men as the

most powerful beings God ever made.
