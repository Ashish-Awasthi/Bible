The empire period

Fifty years after the
fall of Jerusalem,
God sent Cyrus of Persia
to conquer the Babylonians.

Cyrus allowed
all the prisoners in
Babylon to return
to their homelands.

The Exile was over!

Cyrus even paid for the
temple and the walls of Jerusalem to be rebuilt.

Ezra restored God’s
law to the people
and Nehemiah oversaw
the reconstruction.
